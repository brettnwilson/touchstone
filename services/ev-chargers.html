EV Charger FAQs

7 kW and 11 kW are single-phase home chargers. 11 kW adds about 80 km of range per hour. 22 kW is three-phase (commercial supply) and charges twice as fast. Most homes use 7–11 kW overnight charging, which is perfectly adequate.
Possibly. We assess your current electrical capacity. If you have an old fuse box or limited spare circuits, we can upgrade your switchboard first. We often package both together.
A 7 kW charger running 8 hours overnight costs roughly 50–80 cents per full charge, depending on your electricity rate. Much cheaper than public DC fast chargers at $5–15 per session.
