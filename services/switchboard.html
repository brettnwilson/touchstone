Common Issues

Why Upgrade Your Switchboard?

Old fuse boxes pose safety risks. Here's what we solve.

Frequent Tripping

Old fuses blow constantly as homes draw more power than 1970s systems allow.

Fire Risk

Fuse boxes lack modern safety devices. RCDs prevent electrocution and electrical fires.

No RCD Protection

Modern homes need RCD switches on power circuits. Fuse boxes don't have them.

Can't Add Circuits

No room to add new circuits for renovations, EV chargers, or air conditioning.

Insurance & Resale

Old systems complicate home sales, mortgages, and insurance claims.

Won't Support EVs

EV chargers need modern electrical infrastructure. Fuse boxes can't handle it.

What We Install

A modern switchboard is compact, safe, and future-proof. Here's what you get.

  • Main switch for emergency power-off
  • RCD (Residual Current Device) protection on power circuits
  • Overload protection on every circuit
  • Space for new circuits (lighting, power, appliances)
  • Dedicated circuits for high-draw devices
  • Earthing system compliant with AS/NZS 3000
  • Ready for future EV charger installation
  • Weatherproof enclosure with clear labeling
